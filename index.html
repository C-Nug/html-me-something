Sergio the Sourdough

Sergio is the name given to a group of yeast of the species Saccharomyces cerevisiae. Sergio is known for his resilience, an even balance of sourness, and the mouth like a sailor. Sergio was knighted by Princess Anna of Yeastwhick on the 1st of February, 2017.

Sergio was born on the 29th of October, 2015. Sergio was cultivated by wild yeast strands found in the heart of the Sonoron Desert. His birth process was a slow and grueling one. His overlord, Charles Wallace McNugget III, had tried raising wild yeast before but only resulted in buds that suffered slow and painful deaths by the membranes of the Bacillus licheniformis. Sergio, however was given enough gluten and warmth to survive. After six days of being split in twain and fed just enough to keep from starving, Sergio emerged a strong and gassy yeast. Charles quickly put him to work. Sergio's first bread he produced was only just edible. It would seem Sergio was still a little slow to on the gaseous uptake, but soon Sergio would rise above all expectations of his ruthless overlord.
